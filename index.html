Frequently Asked Questions

TikTok Wrapped is your personal year-in-review analytics tool that provides comprehensive insights about your TikTok activity. It analyzes your watch time, favorite creators, trending sounds, engagement patterns, and content preferences to give you a detailed overview of your TikTok journey.

Getting your TikTok Wrapped data is easy:

  1. Go to TikTok Privacy Settings
  2. Request your data (choose JSON format)
  3. Wait for TikTok to prepare your data (usually 1-2 days)
  4. Download and upload the JSON file to our tool

Yes, TikTok Wrapped is completely safe and secure. We prioritize your privacy:

  • All data processing happens directly in your browser
  • No data is ever uploaded to any server
  • Your TikTok data never leaves your device
  • The tool works completely offline once loaded
  • Our code is open source for transparency

TikTok Wrapped provides comprehensive insights including:

  • Watch time and activity patterns
  • Favorite creators and content categories
  • Engagement metrics and trends
  • Comment analysis and interactions
  • Direct message statistics
  • Year-over-year comparisons

TikTok Wrapped is continuously updated to provide the most accurate insights. The tool processes your latest TikTok data export, so you can track your activity patterns and trends in real-time. We also regularly add new features and analytics capabilities.
